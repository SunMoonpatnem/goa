About Us




SunMoon  is a newly opened resort in the southern end of the beautiful Patnem beach in Canacona, South Goa. Surrounded by coconut palm trees on all sides its a peaceful destination. Located 50 meters away, its just a short stroll from the picture-perfect beach but still close enough to hear the waves at night.

The resort offers colourful cozy cottages just perfect for individual, family and friends. Although simple and basically furnished, all the rooms come with western bathrooms and hot showers, double beds, fans and fresh bed linen and towels. Each cottage also has a small veranda with comfy cane chairs.

Our staff, very friendly and helpful are always suggesting activities that can be done or places to visit. Always working to make your stay a memorable one.

The Outs Inn is SunMoon's Bar & Restaurant.We serves best sea food in our retaurant & drinks. Once in a week we organize party, in our restaurant.

Our resort also have yoga classes.for the more details about yoga visit our yoga page.
