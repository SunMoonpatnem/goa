About us


Welcome to the 'The Outs Inn Bar & and Resturaant' of SunMonn Resort of patnem beach of Canacona Goa in South Goa. The Outs Inn Bar &Resturaant has an ever-evolving cocktail list and spectacular views of the sea. Guests can enjoy drinks by sitting in front of beautiful sunset.
Hearty snacks are available alongside the extensive drinks list, including non-alcoholic drinks, as well as an Express Lunch menu. We serves one of the best sea food dishes and Goan specials also. Guests can learn the art of mixology with the popular cocktail masterclasses.
